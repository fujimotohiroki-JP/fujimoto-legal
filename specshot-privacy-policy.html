SpecShot Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FUJIMOTO Hiroki ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile application SpecShot (the "App").

By using the App, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide
The App allows you to import, inspect, and save screenshots and related image data on your device.

b. Locally Stored Data
The App may store screenshots, measurement data, selected settings, and related app data locally on your device for app functionality.

c. Advertising and Device Information
If the App displays ads through Google AdMob or other advertising partners, those partners may collect information such as:

  • device identifiers
  • advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • approximate location
  • app usage and interaction data
  • diagnostic information

d. Analytics and Diagnostics
We may collect limited technical information such as crash logs, performance data, and diagnostic information to improve the App.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide and operate the App
  • save and restore screenshots and app settings
  • enable screenshot measurement and color picking features
  • improve app performance, stability, and usability
  • display and manage advertisements
  • comply with legal obligations

3. Advertising

The App may display ads provided by Google AdMob or other advertising providers.

These providers may use collected information to serve and measure ads, including personalized or non-personalized advertising, where permitted by law.

Depending on your region, we may ask for your consent before showing personalized ads or provide privacy options required by applicable law.

For more information about how Google uses data, please refer to:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Storage

Screenshots and related working data are generally stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated.

We do not claim ownership of screenshots or content you import into the App.

You are responsible for managing, backing up, and deleting your locally stored content.

5. Data Sharing

We may share information:

  • with advertising partners such as Google AdMob
  • with service providers that support app functionality, analytics, or diagnostics
  • when required by law, legal process, or government request
  • to protect our rights, users, or the security of the App

We do not sell your personal information in the traditional sense.

6. Your Privacy Choices

Depending on your location, you may have choices regarding how your information is used, including:

  • consenting to or declining personalized advertising
  • adjusting device-level advertising settings
  • resetting your advertising identifier
  • requesting access to or deletion of certain information where required by law

You may also stop all collection through the App by uninstalling it.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required by applicable law in your jurisdiction.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information related to the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Users

If you use the App outside Japan, your information may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable law and the practices of our service providers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may provide notice through the App or by other appropriate means.

Your continued use of the App after the updated Privacy Policy becomes effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
